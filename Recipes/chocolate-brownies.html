Chocolate Brownies

Description

Rich, fudgy chocolate brownies perfect for a sweet treat.

Ingredients

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 150g butter
  • 200g sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 100g flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Melt the dark chocolate and butter together in a bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the sugar and eggs until light.
  4. Fold in the melted chocolate mixture, then sift in the flour and cocoa powder.
  5. Pour the batter into a greased baking tray and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  6. Let it cool before cutting into squares.
