Recipe 1: Spaghetti Bolognese

Description:

A classic Italian pasta dish made with a rich, meaty tomato sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated parmesan for serving

Steps:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in boiling salted water until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, saut√© the chopped onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add canned tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Let it simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti, topped with grated parmesan.
