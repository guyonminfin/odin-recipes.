Chicken Curry

Description

A flavorful and spicy chicken curry made with a blend of aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 200ml coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pot and saut√© the onion, garlic, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in the tomatoes, turmeric, cumin, coriander, and chili powder.
  4. Pour in the coconut milk and let it simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice.
